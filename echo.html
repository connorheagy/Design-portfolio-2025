Echo
Connect. Listen. Discover.
Echo is a music sharing app that allows users to share and discover new music, building communities one song at a time.
The Idea.
I listen to alot of music, but most of the time I struggle to find anthing new that I truly enjoy. After endless hours of scouring random reddit forums and Spotify playlists, I came up with Echo. At its core, Echo is a music discovery social media app. Echo allows you to post songs that are you are excited about. You can discuss your niche interests in joinable groups that matches your music taste. Echo allows avid music listens to immerse themselves within curated communities. The user interface amplifies the meaning of connection by reducing visual clutter and emphasizing user interaction. Simplifying how we find music, Echo centralizes the task by bringing people together.
The Process.

Research

User interview sessions were conducted during the needfinding phase. This consisted of relevent questions regards user interaction within the context of competitive apps (e.g Spotify). Additionally, participants were given a number of tasks on the platform and observations were recorded for further refinement. Upon compiling quantitative user data, generalized personas were created for accurate implimentation of the product.

Skills

Tools & Frameworks

Design

Encapsuled backend functions to present a digestable user interface.

The Breakdown.

User Interaction

The profile page allows users to view their own profile and see their posts, followers, and following. The user can also edit their profile information and settings. The layout is clean and simple, with a focus on the user's content. The navigation bar at the bottom allows for easy access to the home feed, search, notifications, and profile pages.
